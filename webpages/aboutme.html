About Me

I'm a technical writer and a recent graduate of UMass Amherst. I graduated with an English degree, specializing in Professional Writing and Technical Communication (PWTC). I also have minors in IT and Latin Literature.

I have experience writing software documentation, grant proposals, and more. I have also created designs for magazine spreads, logos, and icons. Check out my Writing and Design pages to see some examples from my portfolio.

In my free time, I love to draw, horseback ride, and play music. I play the saxophone, clarinet, and percussion. I also have a grey tuxedo cat named Bagel and an orange cat named Popcorn.
